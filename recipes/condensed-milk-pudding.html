Condensed Milk Pudding

Description

Simple to prepare, the recipe only uses 4 ingredients that you probably have at home: sugar, condensed milk, milk and eggs. Who wouldn't want to surprise everyone with a smooth pudding? In this recipe you learn the simple and easy technique to achieve a pudding without holes. Check it step by step!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of milk (use the same measure as the condensed milk)
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. In a 20-centimeter diameter pudding mold, place 6 tablespoons of sugar and heat over medium heat until it turns into a caramel syrup (approximately 3 minutes).
  2. Remove from heat and rotate the mold so that the caramel coats the entire bottom and sides. Set aside.
  3. In a blender, combine the sweetened condensed milk, whole milk, and eggs. Blend well for about 1 minute.
  4. Turn off the blender and let the mixture rest for 15 minutes.
  5. During this resting time, the foam will rise to the surface, resulting in a smooth pudding without holes.
  6. Gently remove the foam from the surface using a spoon, and carefully pour the blender mixture into the reserved caramel-coated mold.
  7. Bake in a water bath at 180°C (350°F) for about 1.5 hours.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool, and refrigerate for approximately two hours. Unmold and serve.
