Carrot Cake

Description

Check out this classic, simple and easy carrot cake. It is a very popular sweet, specially for breakfast and afternoon snacks. With its irresistible chocolate topping ans soft, moist interior, it's a recipe that wins everyone's heart

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of oil;
  • 3 medium grated carrots;
  • 4 eggs;
  • 2 cups of sugar;
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of wheat flour;
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder.
  • 1 tablespoon of butter;
  • 3 tablespoons of cocoa powder;
  • 1 cup of sugar;
  • 1 cup of milk.

Steps

  1. In a blender, add carrots, eggs, and oil, then blend;
  2. Add sugar and blend again for 5 minutes;
  3. In a bow or using a mixer, add the wheat flour and then mix again;
  4. Add the baking powder and mix slowly with a spoon;
  5. bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (356°F) for approximately 40 minutes.
  6. Pour the butter, cocoa powder, sugar, and milk in to a bowl, then mix;
  7. Place the mixture on the stove and continue mixing until it reaches a creamy consistency, then pour the glaze over the cake.
